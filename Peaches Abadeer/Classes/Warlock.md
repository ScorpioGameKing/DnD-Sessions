## Class Features
### Hit Points
**Hit Dice:** 1d8 per warlock level
**Hit Points at 1st Level:** 8 + your Con mod
**Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + Con mod per warlock level after 1st

### Proficiencies
**Armor:** Light Armor
**Weapons:** Simple Weapons
**Tools:** None
**Saving Throws:** Wisdom, Charisma
**Skills:** Arcana, Nature

### Equipment
* Spear
* Arcane Focus
* Scholar's Pack
* Leather Armor
* Simple Weapon
### Otherworldly Patron
[[Hexblade]]

### Pact Magic
Your arcane research an the magic bestowed on you by your patron have given you facility with spells.

### Cantrips
You know two cantrips of your choice from the warlock spell list. You learn additional warlock cantrips of your choice at higher levels, as shown in the Cantrips Known column of the Warlock table
