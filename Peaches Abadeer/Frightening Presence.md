As an action, you can exude an aura of terror. Each creature within 10 feet of you and aware of your presence must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your [Charisma](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Charisma "5e SRD:Charisma") modifier) or become [[Frightened]] until the end of your next turn. If your check fails, the target can’t be frightened by you in this way for 1 hour.
