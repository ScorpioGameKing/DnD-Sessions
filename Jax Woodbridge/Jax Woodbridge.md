Features & Traits
---
- Adjust Density:
	- 30 Feet
	- Must be Large or Smaller
	- Weight is Halved or Doubled for up to 1 Minute
	- Half Weight:
		- Speed + 10
		- Jump Doubled
		- Disadvantage on STR Checks and Saves
	- Double Weight:
		- Speed - 10
		- Advantage on STR Checks and Saves
	- Level 10+:
		- Can target Huge or Smaller

- Gravity Well:
	- Whenever you cast a spell on a Creature
	- Can move target 5 Feet to an Unoccupied Space dc 14
		- Target is willing
		- Spell hits it with an attack
		- It fails a saving throw against the spell

- Violent Attraction
	- At 10th level, when another creature that you can see within 60 feet of you hits with a weapon attack, you can use your reaction to increase the attack's velocity, causing the attack's target to take an extra 1d10 damage of the weapon's type.
	- Alternatively, if a creature within 60 feet of you takes damage from a fall, you can use your reaction to increase the fall's damage by 2d10.
	- You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (a minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
	- Max 4 / Current 2

- Darkvision:
	- 60ft Bright
	- Dark is dim light

- Fey Ancestry:
	- Advantage on Charm Saves
	- Magic can't put you to sleep

- Trance:
	- Meditate for 4 hours for full rest

- Feywild Visitor:
	- Harmless visitor when asleep or in deep trance

- Feywild Connection:
	- Friendly Fey are inclined to come to your Aid when in the Feywild

- Distracting Ass:
	- 1d12 CON Save
