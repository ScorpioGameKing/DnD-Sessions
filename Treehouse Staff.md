At first glance, this staff appears to be the branch of an old oak. But upon closer inspection, it is actually a miniaturized tree, with a treehouse nestled within it's branches. Although the origins of this staff are lost to time, it is understood that when the correct magical phrase are invoked it plants itself into the ground and grows large enough for a few people to sleep within it's tiny house.
